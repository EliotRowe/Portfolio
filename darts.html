Design

  • Custom dart barrels were designed in SolidWorks to be turned from 3/8" brass round stock.
  • Dart barrels include a threaded hole on each end to attach screw-on points and shafts.
  • This project is intended as a display peice so creating an interesting aesthetic was prioitized over balance and flight mechanics.
  • Designed custom box with slots cut to hold three darts and standard sized flights.
  • Hinged lid is engraved with my initials and features magnets to hold the box closed.
